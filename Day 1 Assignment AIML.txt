Q1. Explain Machine Learning with your own example?
Ans=>	Machine learning is a Part of artificial intelligence which creates applications that learn from data 
	and improve their accuracy over time.

	data in order to make decisions and predictions based on the data.
Example=> Smart assistants is a machine learning models to interpret speech and provides Result to us.
	  Self-Driving Cars can recognize a object infront of it and take decision based on the circumstances.
